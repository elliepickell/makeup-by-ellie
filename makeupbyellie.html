Summer Makeup Look:


This Blue liner / Pink lip combo is perfect for those bright summer days at the pool with friends.


How To Get This Look:

Step 1: Start with a moisturized face. (I will be using "Hope In A Jar" by Philosophy)
This will help the makeup apply more smoothly.
Step 2: Then, Choose a foundation with a dewy finish.
(I am using Giorgio Armani Luminous Silk Foundation)
This will give the skin some dimension.
Step 3: Line your eyes with the MUFE Aqua Liner.
Step 4: Then apply the MAC Candy-Yum-Yum Lipstick.
Step 5: Then finish off with a little bit of Colour Pop Highlighter in "Smoke N Whistles", And you're done!
